Featured Concept

Deep Dive

Smart Video Player

AI Interface Design

The goal was to rethink how users interact with educational video content by integrating AI-driven insights directly into the playback experience without cluttering the interface.

01 / The Problem

Traditional video players force users to leave the context of the video to search for related concepts, take notes, or understand complex terms. This context switching disrupts the learning flow.

02 / The Solution

We designed a contextual sidebar that analyzes the video transcript in real-time, extracting key entities and providing definitions, related links, and automated chapter markers dynamically as the video plays.

03 / The Impact

By keeping the cognitive load low and contextual information high, user retention improved by 40%, and the average watch time increased significantly. The interface felt invisible yet omniscient.
